How It Works

How to Purchase & Sell SVT

Purchasing SVT Tokens:

Getting started is simple - no need to connect a wallet on the website. Just send USDC on Solana to our predefined Solana address and you'll receive your SVT tokens in less than a minute. It's fast, secure, and straightforward.

Selling SVT Tokens:

If you want to sell your tokens, simply send your SVT (on Solana) to our predefined Solana address and you'll receive USDC on Solana back. Please note there is a 20% service charge to convert from SVT to USDC.

Token Pricing & Distribution Schedule

How the Price is Determined:

The price of each SVT token is defined at the beginning of the 10-week distribution period. Each token entitles the holder to receive 1/1000 of the total income generated during the 10-week period.

Early Buyer Advantage:

Since token holders are purchasing tokens before the distribution period begins, they receive a 54% discount on the token price. This rewards early supporters who believe in the Swissstarnode validator network.

Distribution Schedule:

Profit distributions occur 5 times over the 10-week period, with payments made on 14th December 2025, 28th December 2025, 11th January 2026, 25th January 2026 and 8th February 2026. All distributions are made exclusively in USDC on Solana to ensure stability and ease of use.

Dynamic Pricing:

After each distribution, the token price decreases by 1/5 of the initial price. This declining price model reflects the remaining distribution periods and provides transparent pricing throughout the token lifecycle.

Token Price:

The price of one SVT token for this profit sharing period is 2 USDC. For a detailed breakdown of how this price was calculated, visit the SVT Pricing page.

Distribution Timeline:

  • 14th December 2025: First distribution + Price reduction (1/5)
  • 28th December 2025: Second distribution + Price reduction (1/5)
  • 11th January 2026: Third distribution + Price reduction (1/5)
  • 25th January 2026: Fourth distribution + Price reduction (1/5)
  • 8th February 2026: Fifth distribution + Final price reduction (1/5)
